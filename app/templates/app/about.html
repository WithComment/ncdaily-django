Features

Sorting

The notices are split into two sections. New notices are the unique notices for the day. Past notices are the double-ups.

Email Button

This will email whoever made the notice. It autofills the subject and recepient fields so you save time.

Calendar Button

This will add an event to your calendar with the subject and day filled out. At the moment the time is always 8.15am, but this will be fixed soon.

Search Tool

Use Gmail to search for time, day, location, teacher and event of the notice.
